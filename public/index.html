Who we are

Meet Bukky

Bukola Fabiyi is a health coach, pharmacist, and wellness expert dedicated to helping middle-aged women transform their relationship with food and achieve sustainable weight loss. She guides her clients from restrictive dieting to a balanced, intuitive approach to nutrition and wellness.

With her unique blend of clinical expertise and holistic wellness strategies, she helps clients connect the dots between their lifestyle choices and overall health. Her approach combines evidence-based practices with personalized coaching to create lasting change.

A graduate of the University of Northampton with a Master's degree in Public Health, she brings years of experience in pharmacy and public health to her coaching practice. Her background allows her to bridge the gap between traditional medicine and holistic wellness.

Through her comprehensive approach, Bukola has successfully guided numerous clients toward stronger, healthier bodies and balanced lifestyles. Her work has helped many women achieve sustainable weight loss, improved muscle mass, and enhanced overall well-being.
